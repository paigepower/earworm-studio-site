Recording at Earworm

Are you a band looking for a studio space to record your next album? Are you an individual artist itching for a private studio to get those creative juices flowing? Rent out studio space to record your next project! In our studio, you'll have access to a variety of instruments, microphones, pre-amps, synthesizers, and guitars. Need inspiration? Do you have an idea for a project but just aren't sure how to see it through? Get the help of one of our mixing and recording engineers at an additional cost, who will be with you every step of the way of your project.

Learning at Earworm

Are you interested in learning a new instrument? No experience, no problem! Of course, even if you are experienced, we have a place for you. Our instructors are highly trained and can work with you to discover what your next goals are in your journey. Please refer to our lesson rate chart below for more information.
